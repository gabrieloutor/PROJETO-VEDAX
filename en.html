The Company

About Us

The Vedax is an established company in the market since 1975, specializes in manufacturing Forged Flanges in line, tubular connections and Special Forgings in materials Alloy Steel, Stainless Steel and Carbon, complying with the specifications of domestic and international manufacturing standards.

Our strict quality control standards led us to compliance in all production stages, from the purchase of raw materials to testing laboratories, seeking to ensure that all material characteristics conform to the stipulated standards.

Due to years of experience Vedax acquired know-how in the forging process and heat treatment of Rings, Discs, Flanges and Fittings, based on ASME, API, DNV, among others. This capability was achieved thanks to the technology invested in our manufacturing processes and ability of our employees. This enabled the requisite operating the most stringent requirements, controlling - every manufacturing step - the relevant points, which ensures excellent quality products.
